# AI Meeting Workforce

A multi-agent AI workforce built with Relevance AI that automates the complete meeting lifecycle—from participant discovery and preparation to scheduling, note-taking, and follow-up actions.

## 🚀 Overview

Traditional meetings require significant manual coordination:

* Finding the right participants
* Researching attendees
* Preparing presentations
* Scheduling meetings
* Recording decisions
* Assigning action items

This workforce automates the entire process using a coordinated team of AI agents.

---

## 🏗 Architecture

### Orchestrator Agent

The central decision-maker that:

* Understands meeting requirements
* Determines required participants
* Decides whether supporting materials are needed
* Coordinates all sub-agents

---

## 🤖 Sub-Agents

### Internal Participant Finder

Identifies internal stakeholders using a company knowledge base.

### External Participant Finder

Searches HubSpot contacts and gathers additional context through external research.

### Presentation Designer

Generates meeting presentations automatically using Gamma.

### Meeting Booker

Schedules meetings using Google Meet integration.

### Meeting Note Taker

* Attends meetings
* Records discussions
* Generates summaries
* Extracts action items
* Creates Trello tasks
* Sends follow-up information

## 🔄 Workflow

1. User requests a meeting.
2. Orchestrator analyzes requirements.
3. Internal and external participants are identified.
4. Required research is gathered.
5. Presentation is generated if needed.
6. Meeting is scheduled automatically.
7. Meeting notes are captured.
8. Action items are extracted.
9. Tasks are assigned.
10. Summaries are distributed.
